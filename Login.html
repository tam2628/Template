Stay Safe

We at staysafe.org are a group of energetic and hard-working developers, dedicated in bringing forth a solution to the ever rising issues of urbanisation.

Our intelligent systems provide the latest information concerning safety issues within any city in India, instant access to emergency contacts, and grievance registration.

Using Global Positioning System (GPS), we also provide real time notifications regarding safety issues in your vicinity, all accessible at your fingertips. Our approach will enable citizens to live with awareness and safety in the rapid growing and transforming cities of India.
